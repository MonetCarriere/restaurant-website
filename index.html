We are a modern fine dining restaurant where experimentation becomes the everyday. Every dish at Lush has its own story from traditional recipes to modern updates that tell the history of our team, cooks and chefs who have left their mark on our kitchen as well as our serving staff and managers who have been ambassadors of hospitality. The concept of our restaurant is to bring innovative taste to the table,

from your favourites to those rare varieties you don’t see everyday. Our menu is never done. We find inspiration all around in new ingredients, new approaches, and fresh takes on old standards. Experience cuisine that’s constantly evolving in a setting that’s forever inviting. Drink with delight at the bar. Make the menu your own. Let the exploration unfold.
